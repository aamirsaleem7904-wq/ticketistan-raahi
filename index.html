Raahi v4 · 11-screen journey — design decisions and inspirations
S01 Home — NayaPay inspired · 4 quick-action tiles, single Raahi greeting, "Continue planning" card. Scannable in 2 seconds.
S03 Price prediction — Hopper inspired · 30-day CSS bar chart, colour-coded fare levels, "Book now 87% sure" recommendation. AI made visible.
S04 Flight results — sticky PF bar · Single Price Freeze bar pinned to bottom, not repeated per card. Cleaner, less cognitive load.
S05 CFAR hero — Easypaisa inspired · One full screen, one decision. Big shield icon, 3-stat grid, single toggle. Nothing else competing.
S06 Safar Pro — eDreams Prime inspired · Side-by-side table: this booking PKR 1,308 vs Pro PKR 2,999/yr. Conversion happens in the comparison.
S09 Checkout — Qist total fixed · Shows "6 × PKR 4,401 = PKR 26,407" + "Zero interest — total hamesha same rahega". Murabaha clarity.
S11 My Trips — retention screen · Live countdown, CFAR shield status, full 6-instalment Qist schedule, Raahi shortcut. Shows the product has depth beyond acquisition.
Bottom nav — all 11 screens · Bolder filled SVG icons. Active state: dark navy + teal dot. Correct active tab per journey stage.
